Admissions.

Admission Procedure

Steps :

Students who seek admission can contact our school office on all working days between 9:30am to 3:00pm.
A duly filled-out application with all the required details is to be submitted to the school office.
Students will have to attend an interview with the principal.
Admission for grade 2-9 is done based on a written aptitude test in basic Mathematics and English.
Admissions are finalized based on their performance in the test.

Age Criteria

Criterias are :

Pre-Nursery : 2 years & 6 months as on 1st June of the academic year.
LKG : 3 years & 6 months as on 1st June of the academic year.
UKG : 4 years & 6 months as on 1st June of the academic year.
UKG : 4 years & 6 months as on 1st June of the academic year.

Documents

Carry these documents :

Pre-Nursery : Along with filled application, Aadhar Card copy, Birth Certificate Copy and 3 passport size photographs must be submitted for admission.
1st - 10th Grade : Along with filled application, Aadhar Card copy, 3 passport size photographs, marks card and TC from the previous school must be submitted for admission.
