Facilities.

Audio Visual Rooms

The importance of audio visual technology in education should not be underestimated; hearing through AV creates a stimulating and interactive environment that is more conductive to learning. AV equipments give teachers the chance to stimulate each child’s learning process with a combination of pictures, sound and attention grabbing media.

  • Equipment : High-quality audio and video equipment, including projectors, screens, speakers, microphones, and amplifiers, are incorporated in the AV room.
  • Acoustic Design : Soundproofing and acoustic treatments are incorporated to ensure clear audio and prevent sound leakage.
  • Connectivity : Multiple input and output options for connecting various devices, such as laptops, DVD players, and gaming consoles, are available.
  • Recording and Streaming : Capability to record and stream presentations for archival or remote viewing.
  • Security : Security measures to protect equipment and prevent unauthorized access.

Sports and Recreational Facilities

Games and sports play an integral part in the all-round development of a child RPS has a spacious and secluded playground to facilitate various sports activities, as well as a large indoor area for indoor games. Taking part in sports is important for children, as it reduces stress and enhances their mood. Plying sports helps kids strengthen the immune system, maintaining physical coordination, and enhance body strength.

  • Sports Equipment : Storage and availability of sports equipment like balls, rackets, nets, and other gear for various sports.
  • First Aid Room : A room equipped with basic medical supplies and a trained staff member to attend to sports-related injuries.
  • Multi-Purpose Courts : Some schools have multi-purpose courts that can be used for a variety of sports, including futsal, handball, or dodgeball.

Safe and Reliable Transport Services

RPS has its own fleet of buses which are controlled under GPRS tracking. All the buses are equipped with fire extinguishers and First Aid boxes. We ensure individual seating for all students. Well-trained lady attendants accompany the students on all the buses.

  • Routes and Stops: A list of the established routes and pick-up/drop-off points to help parents plan their child's transportation.
  • Timely Services: Assurance of punctuality and adherence to schedules to ensure students reach school and return home on time.
  • GPS Tracking:Mention of GPS tracking systems in the vehicles for real-time monitoring and enhanced security.
  • Communication Channels:Contact information for the transport service office and emergency contacts in case of any issues or concerns.

Well-Equipped Laboratories

Well-equipped laboratories in schools play a crucial role in providing students with hands-on learning experiences and fostering a deeper understanding of various subjects.it has a dedicated space designed for conducting practical experiments and investigations in subjects such as science, mathematics, technology, and computer science.

  • Lab Instructors:Profiles and qualifications of lab instructors and assistants who provide guidance and support.
  • Science Laboratories: Equipped for physics, chemistry, biology, and environmental science experiments.
  • Computer Labs:Furnished with computers and software for computer science and programming.
  • Interactive Learning: Many labs are equipped with interactive whiteboards, projectors, and audio-visual aids to make learning engaging and interactive.

Library and Resource Center

The Library and Resource Center at RPS School is a vibrant hub of knowledge, providing students with a wealth of resources to support their learning journey. ur library boasts an extensive collection of books, magazines, reference materials, and digital resources to cater to the diverse interests and academic needs of students.

  • Diverse Collection:Our collection includes fiction and non-fiction books, textbooks, academic journals, and e-books, ensuring that there's something for every student.
  • E-Learning Facilities: The library is equipped with computer stations and internet access for students to engage in e-learning and access online educational materials.
  • Librarian Assistance:Our knowledgeable and friendly librarian is available to assist students in finding and utilizing resources effectively.

Health and Wellness Services

At RPS School, we prioritize the health and well-being of our students. Our Health and Wellness Services encompass a range of programs and resources to support their physical and mental health. Our dedicated team of qualified health professionals, including nurses and counselors, is available on campus to provide care and support.

  • Medical Room:We maintain a well-equipped medical room to attend to minor injuries, illnesses, and emergencies during school hours.
  • Health Check-Ups:Regular health check-ups and screenings are conducted to monitor students' overall health and well-being.
  • Emergency Response:We have established clear protocols for handling medical emergencies, ensuring the safety and well-being of students.
